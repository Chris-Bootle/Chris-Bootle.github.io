VFX

Unreal Engine

Brief:

For this assignment, you will be developing a VFX (Visual Effects) collection, consisting of four to five visual effects for a character move set. This collective piece will be built for a character in either a MOBA (League of Legends, DOTA, Smite, etc.) or a Third-Person Hero Shooter (Marvel Rivals, Deadlock), and will focus on using stylised art. You will also be required to build and prototype a demo character for this move set to function on, as well as create a simple demonstration area where the move set collection can be demonstrated. You will be required to make assets in Unreal Engine 5, Substance Designer, and Blender.
